The "Trust Gap" Mechanism

Field interviews (e.g., Gatenga, 3 Dec 2025) reveal a reliance on physical documents due to a lack of digital trust. This creates a cycle of friction and fraud.

1

Manual Submission

Applicants physically carry documents to institutions like AUCA.
Risk: Forgery & Tampering

2

The "Verification Void"

Institutions struggle to verify foreign or local degrees instantly. HEC tightens rules, increasing delays (7+ days).
Risk: Bottlenecks & Corruption

3

Financial Impact

Lack of trusted ID affects SACCOs and employers. Fraud suspects are paraded, but losses are already incurred.
Result: Economic Loss
